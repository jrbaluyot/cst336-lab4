Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system, as well as the closest planet to the Sun. Although closest to the sun, it is not the hottest planet. Mercury's solar day is equivalent to 59 Earth days, and its year is equivalent to 88 Earth days. It also does not have an atmosphere (not really) and is also a terrestrial planet.
