Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It's day is equal to 243 Earth days, and it's year is equal to 225 Earth days. This planet has a thick atmosphere of carbon dioxide gas and clouds of sulfuric acid fill the planet too. It also spins in the opposite direction of Earth and most of the other planets.
